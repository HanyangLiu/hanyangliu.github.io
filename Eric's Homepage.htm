Biography

Call me Eric if you like. I'm currently a research assistant in the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University from July, 2017. I'm working closely with Prof. Kewei Tu on natural language processing and deep learning. Recently I started the research on NLP, such as sequence labeling problems with deep neural networks and graph embedding.

Before that, I received my M. Eng. degree and B. Eng. degree from the School of Automation, Northwestern Polytechnical University, in 2017 and 2014. During my graduate study, I worked closely with Prof. Junwei Han and Prof. Feiping Nie on machine learning and image saliency detection. I've studied in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at National Chung Hsing University (Taiwan), as an exchange student for one semester in 2012.

My CV is here [pdf]. I'm now looking for PhD positions in related research topics.

Research Interest

  • Semi-supervised learning, clustering, subspace learning
  • Sequence labeling for natural language
  • Applied deep learning
